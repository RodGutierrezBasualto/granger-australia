Granger Australia Privacy Statement

Your privacy is important to us.

This privacy statement details the information we gather, how we may use it, and how you can correct or change it if necessary. It relates to the web pages and services available at Granger Australia. We may make alterations to this document from time to time and will update the policy at the site. Granger Australia assumes no responsibility for the information practices of other web sites that a user is able to access through our Sites.

What information do we collect?

The information we collect from you depends on what you're doing. If you enter information on our 'Enquiry' form online, we'll collect personal details such as your name, number, email address, education information and phone number. This information is essential for us to be able to accurately identify you when we access your data. We may collect other information about you in order to help us better serve your needs and requirements but such information will be optional.

How we use your information.

We may use your contact details to send you information about Granger Australia, including material such as information on new products and services we may offer. We will treat the information we collect from you as strictly confidential. Other than as set out below, we will not reveal, disclose, sell, distribute, rent, licence, share or pass on to any third parties, other than those who are contracted or supply services to Granger Australia, any personal information that you may have provided to us unless we have your express consent to do so. The exception to this is where Granger Australia is required by law to provide your information.

We will not share your individual personal information with advertisers but may disclose to them aggregated demographic information in relation to Granger Australia users that does not identify you or your educational requirements. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee the security of any information you transmit to us or receive from our online products or services. Therefore these activities are conducted at your own risk. Once we receive your information, we will use our best efforts to ensure its security. The information we receive from you is kept in a secure environment accessed only by authorised Granger Australia staff.

Data Protection: Your personal information is protected in accordance with Australian Privacy Principles and relevant data protection legislation.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at info@grangeraustralia.com.au or call +61 2 9389 8610.
