We provide expert visa solutions for migrating, working or studying in Australia.

Granger Australia

Established in 1999, Granger Australia is an education and migration consultancy based in Sydney, Australia. We provide a wide range of services for visa solutions specifically tailored to assist you in studying or migrating to Australia.

The migration practice provides expert advice with applications for temporary and permanent residence applications, compliance matters and appeals. Our key areas of expertise are:

  • Employer Sponsored visas
  • Student Visas
  • General Skilled Migration Visas
  • Family and Partner Visas

Our counsellors and registered migration agents have extensive experience in providing high quality education and migration services. With over 20 years industry experience, Granger Australia is a market leader.

Our migration agents are registered with the Australian Government's Office of the Migration Agents Registration Authority (OMARA) & are Members of the Migration Institute of Australia (MIA).

Jonathan Granger

Director, Principal Migration Agent, Granger Australia

Jonathan Granger is the Director/Founder of Granger Australia Pty Ltd. He is a former National President, National Vice-President and NSW/ACT State President of the Migration Institute of Australia (MIA). Jonathan has over 20 years industry experience.

In his role with the Migration Institute of Australia as Board Director and National and State President, Jonathan was actively involved with industry liaison with the Department of Immigration and Border Protection, State Governments and Skills Authorities. In 2012 he received the MIA Service Award for his contribution to "Advance the Migration Profession".

Jonathan regularly presents Continuing Professional Development seminars on Australian Immigration Law for the Migration Institute of Australia. He is also a regular contributor to the MIA submissions to Government and has appeared before the Senate Legal and Constitutional Affairs Legislation Committee to provide expert industry testimony.

As a recognised expert on immigration matters, Jonathan has been interviewed regularly for newspaper, radio and television media including The Australian, The Sydney Morning Herald, ABC News, SBS News, ABC Radio National (Background Briefing), ABC Radio National PM and SBS Television.
