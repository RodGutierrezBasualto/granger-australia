Our Services

Visa & Migration Services

Granger Australia provides expert guidance across all major Australian visa categories. Click on any service below to learn more.

Skilled Migration

Subclass 189 (Independent), 190 (State Nominated) and 491 (Regional). For skilled professionals on the occupation lists seeking permanent or provisional residence in Australia.
Family & Partner Visas

Partner visas (820/801, 309/100), Prospective Marriage (300), Child (802) and Parent (804) visas. Reunite with your family in Australia through the right visa pathway.
Student Visas

Subclass 500 for full-time study at Australian universities, vocational colleges, and English language courses. We assist with institution selection, applications, and pathways to permanent residence.
Business & Investor Visas

Business Innovation (188), Business Permanent (888), and Significant Investor visas. Tailored solutions for entrepreneurs and investors looking to establish or expand business operations in Australia.
Employer Sponsorship

Temporary Skill Shortage (482), Regional Sponsored (494/187) and Employer Nomination (186) visas. We assist both employers and employees through the sponsorship process.
Visitor Visas

Subclass 600 for tourism, business visits, and family visits. Multiple entry options available with various validity periods. We help ensure your application meets all requirements.
