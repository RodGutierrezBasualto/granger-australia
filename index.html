Skilled Workers

For skilled professionals in occupations in shortage. Includes independent skilled migration (189), state-sponsored (190), and provisional regional (491). Requires skills assessment and points test.

Employer Sponsorship (482)

Temporary Skill Shortage visa (Subclass 482) for employers needing skilled workers. Medium-term sponsorship for occupations in shortage. Labour market testing and employer nomination required.

Regional Sponsorship

Permanent or provisional visas (187, 191) for regional Australia. Employer-sponsored positions in designated regions. Pathway to permanent residence with regional employment commitment.

Business Visas

For business owners and investors (188 provisional, 888 permanent). Includes Business Innovation and Significant Investor streams. Substantial investment and business plan required.

Family Visas

Reunite with family in Australia (309, 100, 802, 804). Spouse/partner, child, and parent visas. Sponsorship by Australian citizen or permanent resident family member required.

Student Visas
