Our Principal Agent

Jonathan Granger

Director & Principal Migration Agent

MARA Registration: 0318801

Jonathan Granger is the Director and Principal Migration Agent of Granger Australia, which he founded in 1999. With over 25 years of industry experience, Jonathan is one of Australia's most respected migration professionals.

Jonathan served as National President of the Migration Institute of Australia (MIA) in 2017, and previously held the positions of NSW/ACT State President (2015-2017) and National Vice President (2015-2017). He received the MIA Service Award in 2012 for his outstanding contributions to the profession.

A recognised media expert on immigration matters, Jonathan has been interviewed by ABC, SBS, The Australian, and the Sydney Morning Herald. He has also appeared before the Senate Legal and Constitutional Affairs Committee on migration policy matters.

Jonathan is a NSW Justice of the Peace (#132844) and holds memberships with both MARA and the Migration Institute of Australia.
